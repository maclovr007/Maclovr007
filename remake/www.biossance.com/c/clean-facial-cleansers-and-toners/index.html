Cleanse and prep your skin with Biossances selection of squalane cleanser and squalane toner products. Designed to remove makeup, dirt, and impurities while hydrating and balancing the skin, our cleansers and toners are essential for a healthy routine. Featuring options like our Squalane + Amino Aloe Gentle Cleanser, Squalane + Antioxidant Cleansing Oil, and Squalane + Hyaluronic Toning Mist, these products are crafted with clean, sustainable ingredients to leave your skin feeling refreshed and ready for the next steps in your skincare regimen. Whether youre starting or ending your day, our cleansers and toners ensure your skin stays clear, hydrated, and radiant.


FAQs:

  1. What is a squalane cleanser, and how does it work?
    A squalane cleanser gently removes impurities while replenishing hydration, ensuring your skin feels clean without dryness or tightness.

  2. How does a squalane toner benefit the skin?
    Squalane toner hydrates, balances, and preps the skin for better absorption of serums and moisturizers, leaving it smooth and refreshed.

  3. Can a squalane cleanser be used on sensitive skin?
    Yes, Biossances Squalane + Amino Aloe Gentle Cleanser is suitable for sensitive skin, offering a soothing and hydrating cleanse.

  4. Do I need to use both a cleanser and toner?
    Yes, using both ensures thorough cleansing while balancing and hydrating your skin for optimal results from subsequent products.

  5. Are Biossance cleansers effective for removing makeup?
    Absolutely. Our Squalane + Antioxidant Cleansing Oil is perfect for dissolving stubborn makeup while nourishing the skin.

  6. Can I use a squalane toner as a midday refresh?
    Yes, Biossances toners like our Squalane + Hyaluronic Toning Mist are great for a hydrating pick-me-up throughout the day.
